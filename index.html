Welcome

The Gujarat Innovation Portal is a centralized digital platform to facilitate and promote research, innovation, intellectual property rights, and start-up culture within the state. The portal connects institutions, researchers, and young entrepreneurs with government schemes, funding opportunities, mentorship, and collaboration tools.

Research

Gujarat houses top academic and research institutions contributing to cutting-edge technologies and social innovations. This section provides information on:

  • Government-funded research projects and how to apply
  • Collaborations with industry and academia
  • Success stories and innovation case studies
  • Research labs and incubators across Gujarat

Intellectual Property Rights (IPR)

Protecting your innovations is crucial. This section supports innovators and institutions in:

  • Filing patent, trademark, and copyright applications
  • Understanding the IPR process and benefits
  • Connecting with legal experts and patent agents
  • Tracking the status of your IPR filings

Start-ups

Gujarat is rapidly emerging as a vibrant start-up hub. Whether you're a student, researcher, or entrepreneur, explore:

  • Incubation centers and co-working spaces across the state
  • Seed funding and venture capital support
  • Mentorship programs and startup bootcamps
  • Networking with fellow founders and industry leaders
